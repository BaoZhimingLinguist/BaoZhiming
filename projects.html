I work as PI for the Research Projects

  • **Project Title: Linguistic Markers of Dementia (Principle Investigator)
    **Host Institution: NUS
    *MOE Project*

    - Our main objective is to look for linguistic markers of dementia in natural speech. - We succeeded, and our findings were reported in the paper published in Alzheimer’s and Dementia, which is one of three journals published by the Alzheimer’s Association. It is a major achievement. - For the first time, we discovered significant results based on natural speech data collected from Singaporeans.
